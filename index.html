My name is Isaiah Vallecillo, also known as Ace / AceLazerDesigns. I am a multidisciplinary creative based in Alberta, Canada. A character designer, illustrator, 3D artist, and game developer with a passion for learning and visual design.


Since graduating from the Multimedia Production program at Lethbridge Polytechnic in 2022, I have continued to expand my illustration, 3D modeling, web development and game development skills. My tools of choice include Procreate, Clip Studio Paint, Affinity, Blender, Nomad Sculpt, Godot, Unity and Unreal Engine. I am currently working at Lethbridge Polytechnic as an instructional assistant, helping students to develop their own creative skills.
I want to be able to see parts of myself in my own work, and for others to see themselves in it as well, which is why representation, accessibility and creative intention are at the heart of everything I work in.

Axia

Axia is an original character from a game development project I am working on called RushRiders. With inspirations from the axlotyl as well as the pastel goth fashion aesthetic, she is meant to blend into an early 2000's world which is the setting for my game, RushRiders.

While trying to achieve this low-poly style, I also used a texture atlas with color blocks to keep everything unified, to keep the model optimized and achieve the retro look.
